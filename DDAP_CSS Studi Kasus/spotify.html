Drake's Top Albums

Thank Me Later

June 15th, 2010 >10 million played

Thank Me Later has a languorous, ambient production that incorporates moody synthesizers, sparse beats, obscured keyboards, minor keys, and subtle arrangements. Drake's lyrics are delivered in both rapped and subtly sung verses exploring feelings of doubt, insecurity, and heartbreak.

Listen to Thank Me Later

Take Care

November 15th, 2011 >500 million played

Prior to Take Care, Drake released Thank Me Later, which experienced positive critical success, but left him feeling disjointed about the album's musical content. Drake's vocals on the album feature emotional crooning, alto vocals, a guttural cadence, a melodic flow, and a larger emphasis on singing than on Thank Me Later.

Listen to Take Care Deluxe

For All The Dogs

October 6th, 2023 >600 million played

The album was supported by five singles: "Slime You Out", "8AM in Charlotte", "Rich Baby Daddy", "First Person Shooter" and "You Broke My Heart" for the Scary Hours edition. For All the Dogs performed well commercially, debuting at number one on the Canadian Albums and US Billboard 200 charts, among other countries.
